# Image Assets Required

Please add the following images to the `public` folder:

## Logo
- `public/logo-icon.png` - Logo icon (40x40px or larger, square)

## Hero Section
- `public/hero-bg.jpg` - Hero background image (warehouse/export theme, 1920x500px or larger)

## Product Images (public/products/)
All product images should be approximately 400x250px or larger:

1. `public/products/spices.jpg` - Colorful spices in bowls
2. `public/products/coconut.jpg` - Fresh coconuts
3. `public/products/rice.jpg` - Different types of rice
4. `public/products/moringa.jpg` - Moringa powder/leaves
5. `public/products/exotic-seeds.jpg` - Various exotic seeds
6. `public/products/cow-dung.jpg` - Cow dung cakes/organic fertilizer
7. `public/products/vegetables.jpg` - Fresh vegetables assortment
8. `public/products/nuts-seeds.jpg` - Nuts and seeds mix

## Temporary Placeholders
Until you add actual images, the app will show broken image icons. You can:
1. Use free stock photos from Unsplash, Pexels, or Pixabay
2. Export images directly from your Figma design
3. Use your own product photography

## Image Optimization Tips
- Use JPG format for photos (smaller file size)
- Use PNG format for logos with transparency
- Optimize images before uploading (use tools like TinyPNG)
- Recommended dimensions: 400x250px for product cards, 1920x500px for hero
